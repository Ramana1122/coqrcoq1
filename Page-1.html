Our Story

A culinary journey through the heart of France

"

In the heart of the city, where cobblestone streets whisper tales of yesteryears, coqrcoq stands as a testament to France's culinary heritage.

"

Established in 1987 by renowned chef Philippe Moreau, coqrcoq began as a humble bistro dedicated to preserving traditional French recipes passed down through generations. Today, under the guidance of Chef Isabelle Moreau, we continue to honor those traditions while embracing contemporary culinary innovations.

Our restaurant is more than a dining establishment—it is a sanctuary where time slows down, allowing guests to savor not just exquisite flavors, but moments of connection and joy. Every dish tells a story, every ingredient carefully selected from local producers who share our passion for excellence.

Chef Isabelle Moreau

Executive Chef & Owner
